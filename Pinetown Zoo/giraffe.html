GIRAFFE

Giraffe are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks and legs.

All giraffes have several distinctive features, the most notable of which is their long neck, which allows them to reach high branches and leaves. Their coat is a unique pattern of brown and white patches, which helps them blend into their surroundings. Giraffes are social animals and can often be seen in groups, called towers.
